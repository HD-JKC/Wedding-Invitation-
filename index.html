Connecting Two Hearts Into One

A journey written by Allah before time began.

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said

لَمْ نَرَ لِلْمُتَحَابَّيْنِ مِثْلَ النِّكَاحِ

“There is nothing like marriage, for the two who love one another.”
(Sunan Ibn Majah)

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said

النِّكَاحُ مِنْ سُنَّتِي فَمَنْ لَمْ يَعْمَلْ بِسُنَّتِي فَلَيْسَ مِنِّي وَتَزَوَّجُوا فَإِنِّي مُكَاثِرٌ بِكُمُ الأُمَمَ

Marriage is part of my Sunnah... Get married, for I am heartened by your great numbers.
(Sunan Ibn Majah)

هُنَّ لِبَاسٌ لَّكُمْ وَأَنْتُمْ لِبَاسٌ لَهُنَّ

“Your spouses are a garment for you as you are for them.”
Surah Al‑Baqarah (2:187)

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

وَخَلَقْنَاكُمْ أَزْوَاجًا

“And We created you in pairs.”
Surah An‑Naba (78:8)
